About

I am a hardware-focused engineer specializing in digital RTL design, FPGA prototyping, and hardware security-centric system architecture. My work emphasizes protocol-correct AXI interfaces, timing-clean implementations, and system-level validation on real hardware.

Core Focus

Secure Ethernet datapaths, L2 inspection, ARP/attack detection logic, robust register/control interfaces (AXI-Lite), streaming pipelines (AXI-Stream).

Platforms & Integration

Zynq/Kria bring-up, PS GEM Ethernet, AXI DMA, SmartConnect, IRQ routing (GIC), driver-level debug and performance validation.

Engineering Strengths

Micro-architecture clarity, synthesizable coding discipline, timing closure mindset, verification/debug workflow (simulation + ILA + packet capture).
